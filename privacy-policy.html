Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 3, 2026

1. Introduction

Flight Edge ("we", "our", or "us") operates a flight price comparison service. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our services, including our TikTok integration.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When you connect via TikTok, we receive your TikTok Open ID and basic profile information as permitted by TikTok's API.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our service, including flight searches and preferences.
  • Content Data: Videos and content we publish on your behalf when you authorize posting.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and improve our flight comparison services
  • Post flight deal content to TikTok on your behalf (with your authorization)
  • Send you alerts about flight prices you're tracking
  • Analyze and improve our service

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • TikTok: To publish content on your behalf
  • Service Providers: Who help us operate our service
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law

5. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide our services. You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Revoke TikTok permissions at any time through TikTok's settings

7. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.
